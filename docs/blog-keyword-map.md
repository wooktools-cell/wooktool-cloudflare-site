## SEO Goal

Use blog content to attract B2B buyers who are researching hand tool suppliers before sending an RFQ. Each article should support one commercial page with clear internal links and one quotation path.

Primary conversion pages:

- `/contact/#rfq-form` - RFQ form
- `/tape-measures/` - tape measure category
- `/custom-tape-measure/` - custom tape measure service
- `/utility-knives/` - utility knife category
- `/screwdriver-sets/` - screwdriver set category
- `/oem-odm/` - OEM/ODM service
- `/factory/` - factory proof
- `/quality-control/` - quality-control trust page

## Internal Link Rules

Each article should include:

- 1 link to the most relevant product/category landing page.
- 1 link to `/contact/#rfq-form`.
- 1 trust link to `/factory/`, `/quality-control/`, or `/oem-odm/`.
- 1 to 3 product detail links when the article mentions a specific product type.
- Descriptive anchor text, such as "custom tape measure supplier", not vague text such as "click here".

## Blog Template

Recommended article structure:

1. H1 with the main keyword and buyer intent.
2. Short opening paragraph naming the buyer type.
3. Section: what the buyer should confirm.
4. Section: common mistakes or risk points.
5. Section: customization or packaging options.
6. Section: quality-control checklist.
7. Section: how to send a better RFQ.
8. CTA linking to `/contact/#rfq-form`.

## Publishing Rhythm

Recommended first phase:

- Publish 2 articles per week for 6 weeks.
- Alternate between product keywords and trust/procurement keywords.
- After every 4 new articles, run a sitemap and internal-link scan.
- After Google indexes the pages, review Search Console queries and adjust titles or add FAQ sections.
